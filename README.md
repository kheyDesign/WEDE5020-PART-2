## Project Overview
This project involves the development of a professional website for the Second Chance Pup Foundation (SCPF). Established in 2019 in Centurion, South Africa, SCPF is a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming abandoned and stray dogs. The platform aims to promote animal welfare and encourage responsible pet ownership within the community.

---

## Website Goals and Objectives
The website serves as a strategic tool to:
* **Raise Awareness:** Highlight ongoing rescue and rehabilitation efforts.
* **Showcase Adoptions:** Provide a central platform to display available dogs and adoption procedures.
* **Increase Support:** Encourage donations and volunteer registrations.
* **Community Education:** Share success stories and resources for dog care.

---

## Key Features and Functionality
* **Homepage:** Mission statement and featured rescue stories.
* **About Page:** Overview of the organization’s history and team.
* **Adopt Page:** Comprehensive dog profiles with images.
* **Adoption Enquiry Link:** An embedded link within the "Adopt" section or dog profiles that directs users to an external inquiry form, maintaining a streamlined navigation menu.
* **Get Involved Page:** Integrated options for donations and volunteer sign-ups.
* **Contact Page:** Direct contact details and social media links.
