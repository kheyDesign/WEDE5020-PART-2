Our Journey Since 2019

The Second Chance Pup Foundation (SCPF) was founded in 2019 in Centurion, South Africa, by a dedicated group of animal advocates. From the very beginning, we recognised a critical need in Gauteng for a rescue network that went beyond simply providing shelter. Our vision was to create a safe haven where abandoned and stray dogs could receive both physical care and emotional rehabilitation, giving them the opportunity to heal and thrive.

Today, SCPF stands as a registered non‑profit organisation devoted to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming dogs in need across our communities. Guided by compassion and strengthened by research‑based practices, we continue to grow as a trusted voice for animal welfare, promoting responsible pet ownership and offering every pup the chance at a better life.

Our Vision

We believe in a future where no dog suffers neglect. Our vision is to create communities that value compassion, responsible pet ownership, and animal welfare.

Our Team

From veterinarians to volunteers, our team works tirelessly to provide care, shelter, and hope for every dog we rescue.
